Countries

Holland

Holland is a historical and cultural region located on the west coast of the Netherlands. Since 1840, it is divided into two provinces, North Holland (North Holland) and South Holland (South Holland).

Spain

Spain, also known as the Kingdom of Spain, is a transcontinental country, a member of the European Union, constituted in a social and democratic state of law and whose form of government is the parliamentary monarchy.

Mexico
